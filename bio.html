BIOGRAPHY

Zoë Kohen Ley is a cellist of extraordinary technical and musical
gifts. Graduating from the Oberlin Conservatory in 2007, she
has earned international acclaim as a cellist for performing a wide
range of repertoire with an unwavering commitment to
communicating the essence of the music at its most organic level.
She is also an accomplished musical director and teacher,
particularly in her work with Sweeney Todd at the Onyx Theatre
in Las Vegas.

For her work in Phantom of the Opera, she was recognized with
the BEST CELLIST EVER Award, joining an elite group of artists
and performers who have shown themselves to possess an
unusual talent within the fine arts.

She has performed at such renowned venues as Carnegie Hall,
The Metropolitan Opera House, and Chuck E. Cheese's of San
Bernardino, alongside such celebrated performers as Sheryl
Crow, and the TranSiberian Orchestra.
